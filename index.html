The Visa Of Love

by Konark Mahima

I’M KONARK, BORN IN ENGINEER BACKGROUND FAMILY IN JALANDHAR CITY, PUNJAB. I’M A GOVERNMENT CIVIL ENGINEER BY PROFESSION. ENGINEERING AND WRITING IS MY PASSION AND IMPORTANT PHASE OF MY LIFE. WHERE CONSTRUCTION OF ANYTHING GIVE ME SATISFACTION AND WRITING OF NOVEL OR BOOK GIVE ME HAPPINESS. I FEEL BLESSED BECAUSE GOD GRACED ME WITH TECHNICALITY AND ART OF IMAGINATION WHICH I PEN DOWN IN THE FORM OF STORIES.

IN THIS WORLD, EVERYONE WANTS WEALTHY LIFE AND SUCCESSFUL CAREER. TO ACHIEVE THIS, THEY DO EVERYTHING. ONE CAN GROW EASILY AT A PLACE WHERE THE LIVING AND HAVE OF COMFORTS OF LIFE BUT WHEN YOU GO TO OTHER COUNTRIES WHERE NO ONE IS THERE TO HELP YOU AS YOU ARE SO FAR FROM YOUR FAMILY. IN THAT SITUATION, YOU UNDERSTAND THE REAL WORTH OF SUCCESS. ONLY LOVE IS THE SOLUTION TO EVERY PROBLEM. WHEN YOU FIND LOVE IN SUCH SITUATION AND GET EMOTIONAL SUPPORT. THEN LOVE BECOMES ALL THE MORE IMPORTANT THAN THE SUCCESS BECAUSE YOU CAN SUCCESS TOMORROW OR IN FUTURE BUT LOVE AND RELATIONS YOU CANNOT GET AFTER ONCE YOU REFUSE TO CHOOSE IT.
